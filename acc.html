Travelling and Accommodation

Participants are welcome to arrange their own accommodation. For your convenience, we provide below a selection of hotels located near the conference venue. These hotels offer a range of room types and pricing options, including bed & breakfast arrangements.

Indicative Rates: The prices listed are indicative and based on recent quotations provided to the conference organisers. Please note that actual rates may vary depending on availability, booking dates, and booking platforms.

Participants are strongly encouraged to:
  • Book early to secure better rates and availability
  • Compare prices across different booking platforms (e.g. hotel websites, travel agencies, online booking platforms)

The conference organisers do not have special agreements with the above hotels, unless otherwise stated.
