Travelling and accommodation

Airports

There are two international airports in Cyprus: the Larnaca International Airport (LCA) (about 15-20 minutes from UCLan Cyprus and the Larnaka hotel area) and the Paphos International Airport (PFO) (about 1 hour 30 minutes from Larnaka). The Larnaca International Airport is the recommended and most convenient airport to travel to.

Public transportation in Larnaca area

You can use local public buses for your internal transfers around Larnaka. The official provider, Cyprus Public Transport, offers several ways to plan your journey and navigate the city.

  • Website: For routes, timetables, and general information, visit www.publictransport.com.cy
  • Mobile App: Download the Pame App for real-time information, route planning, and navigation via an interactive bus map. Available on Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store).
  • On the Website: You can also use the "Plan your trip" feature to find the best routes between your hotel and UCLan Cyprus campus.

For those who prefer transfer by car, town taxis are widely available in Larnaka.

Food & Drinks, other expenses

Larnaka offers a wide variety of dining options, from traditional Cypriot taverns along the famous Mackenzie Beach strip to restaurants in the city center and Finikoudes promenade. A group dinner arrangement will be confirmed in the final agenda.

Things to do & places to visit

Larnaka is a vibrant coastal city with a rich history and beautiful seafront:

  • Finikoudes Promenade: A palm-tree lined seafront with cafes, restaurants, and a Blue Flag sandy beach.
  • Mackenzie Beach: A trendy area near the airport, famous for its beach clubs, seafood taverns, and lively atmosphere.
  • Larnaka Salt Lake: A natural habitat for flamingos (winter/spring) and home to the iconic Hala Sultan Tekke mosque.
  • St. Lazarus Church: A magnificent 9th-century Byzantine church in the city center.
  • Larnaka Marina: A modern marina with luxury yachts, shops, and dining options.

You can get all info about Cyprus and places to visit by clicking on www.visitcyprus.com.
