UCLAN Cyprus

The conference will be hosted at the University of Central Lancashire Cyprus (UCLan Cyprus), located in Pyla, in the Larnaca district. Established in 2012, UCLan Cyprus is the first British university campus on the island, combining UK academic standards with a modern Mediterranean setting.



Village of Pyla

Pyla is a historic village northeast of Larnaka with continuous habitation since the Bronze Age. It remains one of the few mixed communities of Greek Cypriots and Turkish Cypriots.
