Œ£tatMod through the years...

Statistical Modeling with Applications (Œ£tatMod2026) is an international scientific conference dedicated to advancing the theory and practice of statistical modeling. Founded in 2020, the conference brings together leading researchers and professionals from around the world exploring both theoretical foundations and real-world applications of modern statistical methods. The conference has been hosted in multiple European countries, including, France (Rouen), Romania (Bucharest), Serbia (Belgrade), and Greece (Piraeus). The goal of the conference is to encourage collaboration across a variety of disciplines as well as discussion on recent challenges and innovations in the broader area of statistical modelling.
