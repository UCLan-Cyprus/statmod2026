Venue/Location

The conference will be hosted at the University of Central Lancashire Cyprus (UCLan Cyprus), located in Pyla, in the Larnaca district. Established in 2012, UCLan Cyprus is the first British university campus on the island, combining the academic standards of the UK higher-education system with a modern Mediterranean setting. The campus features contemporary lecture theatres, seminar rooms, a spacious auditorium, and dedicated networking areas, providing an ideal environment for academic exchange and collaboration. UCLan Cyprus is situated approximately 15 minutes from Larnaka International Airport and is easily accessible via major transport routes.
